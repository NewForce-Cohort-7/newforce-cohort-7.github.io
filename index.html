Cohort Three

Cohort Three was our first ever completely remote cohort due to Covid-19. These intrepid grads were in class from 9 to 4 every weekday on Zoom. Over the course of the six-month program, they dove headfirst into front-end and server-side development and gained experience working on remote teams. They're a group of great communicators, fast learners, and creative problem solvers. They're ready to make an impact on your team!

About NewForce

NewForce is a six-month, full-time, immersive software development training program. We train our students how to learn and how to work on teams so that they're ready to hit the ground running on day one of their first dev job, even in a tech stack we didn't teach them.
